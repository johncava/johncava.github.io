About Me

I am a 4th year Ph.D Student in the School of Computing and Augmented Intelligence at Arizona State University. I am currently being co-advised by Ross Maciejewski and Abhishek Singharoy. I received my B.S degrees of Computer Science, Mathematics, and Molecular Bioscienecs & Biotechnology from ASU in 2018. My research interests include Deep Learning and their applications to Molecular Biology.
